Occasionally, we see images of food that look similar to what we have eaten before but we are not sure about the name of the dish. We came up with an idea to identify food names with an image using classification algorithms. Here the users can use the web app easily by uploading an image of a dish and getting accurate/predicted feedback on what the food is and getting the nutritional facts behind the dish.
